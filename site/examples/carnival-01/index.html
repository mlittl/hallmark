The three artists on Cold Snap

01.

June Belmont

Detroit  ·  Vocals  ·  Synth

Two tracks. The first is the title track. The second is the one we played first when we listened back and immediately fought over who got to sequence the EP.

Lead single Cold Snap 3:42

02.

Wax Theatre

Brooklyn  ·  Guitar  ·  Tape loops

Two tracks recorded over one weekend at the practice space on Smith Street. Live, no overdubs. The amp hum is the second instrument and we kept it in on purpose.

Track B / C Long January 4:18

03.

Margot Half-Step

Chicago  ·  Piano  ·  Voice

Two tracks. One is the EP closer. Margot's voice goes places we did not expect when we signed her. We are happy about it. So is everyone we played the rough mixes for.

Closer Light Out 5:06
