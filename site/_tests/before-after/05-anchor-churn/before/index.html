The hidden leak

Churn is a symptom. Most companies treat it wrong.

Most SaaS teams respond to churn with discounts and winback campaigns. These slow the bleeding but never address the wound. Customers leave because of gaps in value delivery — and no outreach campaign fixes a broken experience.

Anchor goes upstream. We identify the signals, segments, and product moments that predict departure, then build systems that intervene before customers ever consider leaving.

Reactive retention is a tax
Every save motion — CSM calls, email sequences, discount offers — costs real money and bandwidth. You're spending to retain customers you should have never lost.
The data is already there
Product usage, support tickets, and NPS responses contain clear churn signals. Most companies lack the framework to surface and act on them in time.
Churn compounds faster than growth
At 5% monthly churn, you lose 46% of your base in a year. No acquisition strategy can outrun that math without fixing retention first.
