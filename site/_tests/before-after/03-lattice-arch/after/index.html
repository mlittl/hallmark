Practice

We work exclusively on homes that already exist. Renovation is not a compromise — it is an argument for the life already built into a place. We are slow and deliberate: each project takes what it takes, no longer and no shorter.

Our practice is rooted in Lisbon but not limited to it. We have worked from the Alfama to the Alentejo. What we carry with us is the same conviction: that materials speak, and that good light is non-negotiable.

Our approach

01

Listen
first.

Two or three visits before a single line is drawn. We start with the house as it is, not as we imagine it should be.

02

Material
before form.

The plaster, the tile, the timber — we choose materials that age with the building, not against it.

03

One project
at a time.

We limit active work to four projects. Each receives full attention, every week it is with us.
