City we know deeply

Lattice was founded in 2018 on a single conviction: that residential renovation in Lisbon deserved the same rigour applied to new construction — perhaps more.

Lisbon's housing stock is extraordinary. Centuries of building culture — craftspeople who understood lime plaster, hydraulic tile, and timber floors — left an inheritance that most contractors treat as an obstacle. We treat it as the brief.

We are a small practice by design. Every project has a principal architect present from first conversation to final walk-through. We take on fewer projects than we could, because the work requires it.

Our office is on Rua de São Bento, in the kind of building we most enjoy working on — one that has been altered, patched, and loved by several generations, and still has more to give.
