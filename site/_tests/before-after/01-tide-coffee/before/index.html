The process

Intentional, from
field to cup

01

We source directly

Every month our team selects a single farm or cooperative that meets our quality and ethics standard. No blends, no brokers, no shortcuts.

02

Roasted to order

We don't pre-roast and store. Your bag is roasted the week of your ship date, ensuring peak freshness on arrival — not weeks later.

03

Ships in 48 hours

Once roasted, your order ships within 48 hours. Whole bean or ground, with tasting notes and brewing guidance enclosed.
